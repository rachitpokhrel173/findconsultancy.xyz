Australia Student Visa Requirements for Nepali Students

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from a CRICOS-registered provider
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement — very important
  • English proficiency: IELTS 6.0+ overall, no band below 5.5
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)
  • Proof of funds: minimum AUD 21,041/year
  • Valid passport and academic transcripts
💡 Nepal visa grant rate is approximately 87%. A strong GTE statement explaining why you chose Australia and your genuine ties to Nepal is the most important part of your application.
