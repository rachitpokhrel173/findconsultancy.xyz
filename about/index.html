About FindConsultancy.xyz

We exist to protect Nepali students from consultancy fraud and help them make informed decisions about studying abroad.

Our Mission

Every year, thousands of Nepali students are cheated by unregistered or fraudulent consultancies. They pay Rs. 50,000–2,00,000 to agents who disappear, forge documents, or make illegal visa guarantees. We built FindConsultancy.xyz to fix this.
