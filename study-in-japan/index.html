Japan Student Visa Requirements for Nepali Students

  • Certificate of Eligibility (CoE) issued by Japanese immigration
  • Acceptance from a Japanese language school or university
  • Japanese proficiency: JLPT N5 minimum (N4 recommended)
  • Bank statement showing JPY 1,500,000+ in savings
  • Letter of guarantee from financial supporter in Nepal
💡 Most Nepali students start at a Japanese Language School for 1–2 years before entering university. Specialist consultancies like Orient Consultancy and Shin Japanese School can guide this pathway.

JLPT Levels You Need

  • JLPT N5 — Basic level, sufficient for initial application
  • JLPT N4 — Recommended before departure to Japan
  • JLPT N2 — Required for most Japanese university programs
