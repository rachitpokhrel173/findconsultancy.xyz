Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

FindConsultancy.xyz collects minimal information to operate the platform:

  • Scam Reports: Name of consultancy, location, complaint type, and optional contact information you choose to provide.
  • Premium Listing Inquiries: Consultancy name, contact person name, phone, email, and payment transaction ID.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics via Google Analytics (page views, sessions, location data at city level only).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process premium listing applications and activate listings within 24 hours
  • To investigate scam reports and publish appropriate alerts
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns
  • To contact you regarding your submission if needed

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require user accounts or login
  • We do not collect credit card or payment card details (payments handled via eSewa/Khalti)
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing

4. Scam Reports

Scam reports submitted through our platform are reviewed before publication. The reporter's identity and contact information (if provided) is kept strictly confidential and never published. Only the consultancy name, location, and nature of complaint may be published.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data including pages visited, time on site, and approximate location (city level). You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

6. Data Retention

Listing application data is retained for as long as the listing is active plus 12 months. Scam report data is retained indefinitely for public safety purposes.

7. Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact us at hello@findconsultancy.xyz.
