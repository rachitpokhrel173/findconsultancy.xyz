🇯🇵 Study in Japan

Affordable tuition, part-time work rights, world-class technology education, and a booming job market.

Why Japan?

Japan is a fast-growing destination for Nepali students. Lower tuition than Western countries, 28 hours per week work permission, and increasing demand for foreign workers make Japan a practical, affordable path.

800+ Universities

Much cheaper than English-speaking countries

🗣️

Japanese Required

JLPT N5/N4 minimum for most programs

Visa Requirements

  • Certificate of Eligibility (CoE) from Japanese immigration
  • Acceptance from a Japanese language school or university
  • Japanese proficiency: JLPT N5 minimum
  • Bank statement: JPY 1,500,000+ in savings
  • Letter of guarantee from financial supporter
💡 Most Nepali students start at a Japanese Language School for 1–2 years. Consultancies like Orient Consultancy and Shin Japanese School specialise in this pathway.

JLPT Levels

  • JLPT N5 — Basic level, sufficient for initial application
  • JLPT N4 — Recommended before departure
  • JLPT N2 — Required for most Japanese university programs
