🇰🇷 Study in South Korea

Government scholarships, low tuition, and a growing demand for international workers in Asia's 4th largest economy.

Why South Korea?

South Korea has emerged as an attractive destination for Nepali students seeking affordable, quality education in Asia. With generous GKS scholarships, thriving K-culture, and a strong economy, Korea offers excellent value.

Visa Requirements (D-2)

  • Acceptance letter from a Korean university
  • D-2 visa application at Korean Embassy Kathmandu
  • Korean or English proficiency depending on program
  • Bank statement: KRW 5,000,000+
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
💡 GKS Scholarship fully funds tuition, accommodation, monthly stipend, and airfare. Applications open annually — ask your consultancy about deadlines.
